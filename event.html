Jury & Awards

The best acts decided by the International JURY from the Preliminary round will compete again in the Final Round in front of lay audience and the international jury. The Grand Prix winner will receive the Golden Cat Award and a 1000 levs cash check from our sponsors – “Marina” LTD.

About the Gathering

The end of March will be magical and inspiring for both professional and hobby magicians, anticipant to understand who will be the new Golden Cat awarded winner.

Everyone who is already under the spell of The Golden Cat, knows how much someone not brave enough to come, will lose. The Golden Cat is becoming bigger and better with every year and to prove that the organizers from Quick Hands project and The Municipality of Gabrovo have prepared unforgettable line-up of Star Magicians.
